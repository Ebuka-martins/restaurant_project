About Phantom Restaurant

Welcome to Phantom Restaurant

Located in the heart of Dublin City, Phantom Restaurant has been serving exceptional cuisine since 2020. Our commitment to culinary excellence and outstanding service has made us one of Dublin's most beloved dining destinations.

Our Cuisine

We pride ourselves on creating innovative dishes that blend traditional Irish ingredients with contemporary cooking techniques. Our menu changes seasonally to ensure we're always serving the freshest, highest-quality ingredients available.

Dining Experience

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a casual meal, our atmospheric dining room and attentive staff ensure every visit is memorable. Our sophisticated yet welcoming environment provides the perfect setting for any dining occasion.

Our Team

Led by Executive Chef Martins Ebuka, our team of passionate culinary professionals is dedicated to creating exceptional dining experiences. From our kitchen staff to our service team, every member contributes to making your visit special.
